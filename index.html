This is Romeo

A Web Designer & Software Developer

Romeo is a web designer and software developer living in New Zealand. Since he loves the culture and people of New Zealand, he came to New Zealand from the mainland of China in 2016 to study and pursue his career, and New Zealand is his second home.

He graduated from the University of Canterbury with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science in 2021. The internet field has opened the door to a new world and created many opportunities for him.

He enjoys exploring the differences in the world, meeting like-minded people, and is eager to learn new things. In his spare time, he likes to learn and study new areas of content such as video creation and new concepts of the future.

During his time at the university, he worked diligently to learn and immerse himself in the local culture, laying the foundation for his path to web software development.

In his leisure time, he enjoys the delight of collecting various tutorials and books to enrich his mind, and he always comes up with different approaches to fulfil his goals.
